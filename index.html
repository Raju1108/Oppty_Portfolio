Clear Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Transparent answers about our engineering methodology, delivery stages, and pricing structures.

What is your typical project delivery timeline?

For custom corporate or landing sites under our Business Package, delivery spans 4 to 6 weeks. Highly complex enterprise web applications requiring backend architecture or extensive database integrations can take 8 to 12 weeks depending on the exact wireframe requirements.

Will our marketing team be able to edit page content easily?

Yes. We integrate modern headless CMS panels (like Sanity.io or Strapi) that decouple the structural code from visual content editors. This allows your sales and marketing teams to publish blog entries, update statistics, or edit service text instantly without touching a single line of code.

Do you offer custom backend API and database development?

Absolutely. We build fully secure custom API controllers (Node.js/Express or FastAPI) and scale-tested relational PostgreSQL or Cloud SQL databases. This is a core focus of our Enterprise Package, ensuring safe, rapid operations even under heavy multi-department usage.

How does the support retainer cycle operate?

Our monthly retainer or AMC contracts allocate direct weekly development hours dedicated to monitoring, threat scanning, asset compression, database backups, and prompt minor layout updates. Think of us as your external, on-demand Chief Technology Officer.

What happens if our user traffic spikes suddenly?

Because we architect our platforms using modern decoupled frontend delivery networks (Vercel Edge, Cloud Run) and optimized Redis caching, your pages scale dynamically to absorb sudden surges. This guarantees a consistent sub-second loading experience for all users.
